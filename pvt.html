The Psychomotor Vigilance Task, or PVT, is a well established test of sleepiness. Currently, to do it, you have to buy a box from the 80's that costs hundreds of dollars. I helped some UW researchers in programming an Android app to make this test available to anyone.

Published in Pervasive Health 2013

Technology: Android
Collaborators: Matt Kay, Julie Kientz, and many others (see publication above)
Date: Summer 2011
