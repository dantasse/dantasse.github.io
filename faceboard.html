FaceBoard

The lobby of the HCII doesn't have a directory. It does have a projector with a Kinect hooked up to it that detects touches. We turned this into an interactive directory.

Technology used: Processing, hand-made Kinect library by former HCII students.
Collaborators: Chris MacLellan, Brandon Taylor, Tatiana Vlahovic, Jenny Olsen, Dave Gerritsen, Erik Harpstead, Robert Xiao, Beka Gulotta
Date: August-September 2012
