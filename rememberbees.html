RememberBees

It's happened hundreds of times: you're introduced to someone and, five seconds later, you've forgotten their name. RememberBees, an Android app, aims to fix that. When you meet someone, you hit your phone. It then buzzes you a few times over the next few minutes, each time reminding you to rehearse the person's name.

Some thoughts about this:
Time spacing of the reminders
Small interactions
Whack Gestures, Hudson et al 2010, used as a triggering mechanism

Technology used: Android
